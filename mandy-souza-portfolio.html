Strategic vision & execution

I am a multidisciplinary designer focused on methodology, learning experiences, and collaborative innovation. My work sits at the intersection of strategy and making — translating complex ideas into structured processes and tangible outcomes.

I'm particularly interested in how design can create clarity within complexity, whether through visual frameworks, facilitation tools, or rapid prototyping. I enjoy building environments where people can think critically, experiment confidently, and transform ideas into actionable solutions.

"My practice combines systems thinking, co-creation, and hands-on experimentation — always balancing strategic vision with practical execution."

Based in Gothenburg, Sweden, I work with cultural projects, educational institutions, NGOs, and innovative organizations in Brazil and internationally.

Projects & experience

2023
Facilitation Methodology

Urban Co-Creation Hackathon

Recife, Brazil

Co-designed and facilitated the methodology for a 3-day urban innovation hackathon — creating structured tools to guide teams from research to prototyping, mentoring participants, and supporting digital fabrication.

The project

A 3-day urban innovation sprint focused on reactivating public life in downtown Recife. The challenge invited multidisciplinary teams to develop creative, feasible, and sustainable solutions for Rua Nova — a historic commercial street undergoing urban transformation. The initiative combined strategic urban thinking, collaborative design, and digital fabrication.

My role

I contributed to the strategic design of the hackathon methodology and the overall participant journey — structuring a process that balanced creative exploration with clarity and execution.

  • Structured observation and ideation canvas
  • Step-by-step methodology cards to guide teams through each phase
  • Visual support materials to align decision-making and focus

During the sprint, I mentored teams in refining insights, defining opportunity areas, and assessing feasibility. I also guided participants in materializing concepts using digital fabrication tools.

Digital Fabrication Practice

3D Printing, Laser Cutting & Creative Technology

End-to-end digital fabrication work — from 3D modeling and file preparation to machine operation and physical production. Designing and facilitating courses that make fabrication technologies accessible and strategically applicable.

The practice

I work with digital fabrication as both a technical and strategic tool, integrating 3D printing and laser cutting into processes of experimentation, prototyping, and product development. My practice connects digital design with physical production, enabling fast iteration and tangible validation of ideas.

What I develop

  • Functional prototypes for innovative solutions
  • New products designed for end users
  • Educational tools and learning resources
  • Signage systems and spatial communication elements

I design and deliver courses and workshops on digital fabrication for diverse audiences — focusing not only on how to use the machines, but on how to apply them to solve real-world challenges.
